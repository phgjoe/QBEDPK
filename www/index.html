Why QBE

QBE has a long history of making it possible for key partners to turn their business ambitions and plans into reality.

We are solution driven and strive to overcome challenges so that you can be successful and profitable.

We are committed to developing a mix and diversity of products and offerings that give you a competitive edge.

We also take a holistic view of product distribution, with U.S. Middle Market Vice Presidents and business advisors who cover all lines, making it easier for you to do business with us.

Our agency partners benefit from independently minded local experts, dedicated underwriting and claims handling, backed by the security and strength that comes from our global presence and more than 125 years of leadership in insurance. Most importantly, we are rated “A” (Excellent) by A.M. Best and “A+” by Standard and Poor’s.*

* For ratings guidelines and the latest information, access ambest.com and standardandpoors.com

About QBE > QBE US Middle Market

QBE US Middle Market

U.S. Middle Market is an intermediary distribution channel that works with independent and exclusive agents across the country.

Our model

  • Franchise value – the right agents in the right places
  • Committed to independent agency distribution channel
  • Local presence with dedicated underwriting and claims handling
  • Knowledgeable sales experts
  • Loss control expertise
  • Ebiz solutions for personal lines and small business
  • Outstanding claims handling
  • Manage risk and propose reduction, retention and reinsurance options
  • Target “best in class” risks in a variety of classes
  • Offer a wide range of products for personal and small commercial lines, agri and large accounts
  • Individual account flexibility

We want to do business with insurance partners who have a “make it possible” attitude and track record of results.

Innovation with QBE Connect®

We bring together three key stakeholders – the injured, the employer and their agent, and the medical professional – in a collaborative environment to determine the best way the employee can return to work. The process includes:

  • Responsive and thorough investigation
  • Fair and accurate return-to-work approach
  • Emphasis on risk management
  • Proactive account management
  • Case conferencing for direct involvement of key stakeholders
